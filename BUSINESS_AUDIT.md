## Part 2: Pricing & Monetization

### Current Pricing
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | £0 | Watermarked documents, basic generator |
| Pro | £9/mo | Clean documents, auto-updates when laws change |
| Agency | Hidden | White-label for clients |

### Issues
1. **No annual billing discount** — £9/mo = £108/yr. Offer £79/yr (save 27%) and you front-load cash.
2. **Agency tier has no public price** — agencies won't engage without knowing the cost.
3. **Free tier has no email wall** — biggest revenue leak. Require email to view the generated document.
4. **No upsells:** No priority support, no custom legal review, no API access, no multi-user.
5. **No one-time purchase option** — some users want to pay once for a single policy.

### Recommended Pricing
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | £0 | Generate 1 policy (email required), watermarked |
| Pro Monthly | £12/mo | All docs, clean export, auto-updates |
| Pro Annual | £79/yr | Same as Pro but £6.58/mo equivalent |
| Agency | £49/mo | White-label, 10 client slots, API access |
| Enterprise | £199/mo | Unlimited clients, priority support, custom legal review |

**LTV Calculation:**
- Current: £9/mo × 12mo × 8mo avg retention = **£72 LTV** (terrible)
- With annual billing: £79 × 14mo retention = **£92 LTV** (better, but still low)
- With Agency tier: £49 × 12mo + £79 base = **£667 LTV** (transformative)
- With Enterprise add-ons: could hit **£1,500+ LTV**

### Additional Revenue Streams
1. **Affiliate program** — 30% recurring commission on referred customers
2. **WordPress plugin** — £49 one-time or £5/mo subscription
3. **Shopify app** — £9.99/mo on Shopify App Store
4. **API access** — £29/mo for white-label API generation

1. ✅ "I actually need a privacy policy" — site copy handles this well
2. ⚠️ "PolicyGen is as good as a lawyer" — **not proven anywhere on the site**
3. ❌ "This company is legitimate and won't disappear" — No Trustpilot, no G2, no testimonials

## Part 5: Product & Feature Gaps

### 🔴 Critical (Blocking Revenue)
1. **Email capture on free tier** — Free users generate and leave. No retargeting possible.
2. **No annual billing** — Leaving £30+/customer on the table.
3. **Hardcoded counter** — "3,847 docs" undermines all trust claims.

### 🟠 High Impact (Blocking Growth)
4. **Agency tier pricing hidden** — Second highest-LTV customer segment can't evaluate.
5. **No WordPress plugin** — 43% of websites run WordPress. Massive TAM.
6. **No Shopify app** — E-commerce = highest compliance need. Ready-made distribution.
7. **Only English** — EU market wants GDPR docs in German, French, Spanish, etc.
8. **No document editing** — Users can generate but can't customize after the fact.
9. **No multi-user accounts** — Agencies need to manage client docs.
10. **No API** — Developers want to integrate generation into their own workflows.

### 🟡 Medium Priority (Polish)
11. Only 1 blog post — thin content signal to Google
12. Compliance scanner doesn't render JavaScript — misses modern SPAs
13. No dark mode — but the vintage design doesn't need it
14. No email notifications when regulations change
15. Document export is TXT only — should support HTML, PDF, Markdown
16. No A/B testing on CTAs or pricing page

### 🟢 Quick Wins
17. Add "Last updated: X days ago" to pricing page (social proof)
18. Add a "vs Competitors" page (captures comparison traffic)
