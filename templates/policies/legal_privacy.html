Privacy Policy — PolicyGen

Last updated: April 6, 2026. This policy applies to the PolicyGen website and services (policygen.ai).

1. Who We Are

PolicyGen ("we," "our," "us") operates the policygen.ai website and generates legal policy documents for users. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

When you use PolicyGen:

  • Account data: Email address, username, password hash
  • Generated documents: Company name, website URL, contact email, industry, and compliance preferences you enter to generate policies
  • Compliance checker: When you scan a URL, we fetch that page and store basic compliance results (score, issues found)
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, session identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data to: provide the policy generator service, save and display your generated documents, power the compliance scoring tool, improve the platform, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Billing Data

If you subscribe to a paid plan, we process payments through Stripe. We do not store your payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing: stripe.com/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with: Stripe (payment processing), our hosting provider (Railway), and when required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain generated documents for as long as your account is active. You may delete documents at any time. Upon account deletion, all associated data is removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, UK Data Protection Act, and similar laws, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion ("right to be forgotten"), export your data in a portable format, and object to processing.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@policygen.ai.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use analytics or tracking cookies without your consent. See our Cookie Policy.

9. Security

We use HTTPS encryption, secure session handling, and follow Django security best practices. Passwords are hashed using Django's PBKDF2 algorithm.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email or a prominent site notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For any privacy questions: privacy@policygen.ai
